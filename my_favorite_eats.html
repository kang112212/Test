My Favorite Restaurants

Depending on your location your experience will differ. I was blessed with going to an amazing location one day. The Zucchini was sauted perfectly and I enjoyed eating artichoke for the very first time. A very positive experience.

Via is an extremely upscale resteraunt in Worcester MA.

Worcester Pizza Factory is my favorite place to get pizza. They cook everything to perfection!

Silly's is a vegan resteraunt in Portland ME. Amazing craft beers and dishes.

Olive Garden is a classic. Some love it for its endless breadsticks. I definitely do.
